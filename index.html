How to open Vs Code: You go to the documents section on your computer and go to the donloads file. From there, you left click on the correct download and open Vs Code to start coding. Setting it up, you have to add the html, css, and js to type the codes in. Then you continue with what you want to code.

Console screens are used to check if your codes are correct or not so that you can debug easily what is wrong. To open the console screen, you left click on the page and scroll down to inspect, you click on it and then on the top bar, it will say the console component and if you click on it you will open the console screen.
